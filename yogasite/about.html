About Nicole

Nicole Mishler is an international yoga teacher, actress, writer, and entrepreneur. On a mission to make tools for mental, emotional, and physical health, she provides high quality practices on yoga and mindfulness to support and inspire people of all ages, ethnicities, and backgrounds across the globe.

With a deep reverence and respect for yoga philosophy, Nicole combines studies of traditional yoga with developmental movement, storytelling, and mindfulness application for the modern day. Her approach to guiding regular self care embodies a playfulness, wisdom, and humor that breaks barriers amidst wellness culture and encourages all shapes and personalities to the mat. She was certified in Austin, TX and has gained extended training from master teachers across the United States for over 15 years.
